Interpretation Guide
============================================================

BINARY METRICS (Task 1):
  - Accuracy > 0.90: Excellent overall performance
  - F1 > 0.85: Good balance of precision and recall
  - High Precision, Low Recall: Conservative (misses hate speech)
  - Low Precision, High Recall: Aggressive (false positives)

CATEGORY METRICS (Task 3):
  - Accuracy > 0.80: Good multi-class performance
  - Macro F1: Equal weight to all categories
  - Weighted F1: Weighted by category frequency
  - Check confusion matrix for category-specific issues

TOKEN COVERAGE (Task 2):
  - Mean Coverage 0.2-0.4: Typical for hate speech detection
  - High coverage (>0.5): Liberal extraction
  - Low coverage (<0.1): Conservative extraction
  - Compare with binary detection for consistency

BEST PRACTICES:
  1. Use multiple models for comparison
  2. Validate predictions on a held-out test set
  3. Manually review misclassifications
  4. Consider domain-specific fine-tuning for your use case
